BIP 148: Mandatory activation of SegWit deployment.

User Activated Soft Forks allow the economic majorty of users to specify new behaviors that the miners must then enforce.

You may wish to read the formal specification for BIP 148, and here for more conext on why many are planning to enforce the activation of SegWit.
